THE TACTICAL SHIFT

My name is Aaron, hailing from the Philippines. I've been immersed in the world of Valorant since 2023. In the beginning, I relied heavily on raw mechanical aim to dominate my matches.

Now in my 30s, I've undergone a significant evolution. I've shifted my focus to the tactical mastery of the game. I trust my teammates implicitly and prioritize strategic positioning over flick shots, recognizing that while reflexes might fade, game sense only sharpens with time.

I am passionate about coaching to leave a lasting impact on the scene.
